# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want SurpriseSage to be a welcoming project for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Be kind.** Treat others the way you'd want to be treated.

**Be constructive.** Feedback should help, not hurt. Suggest improvements, don't just criticize.

**Be patient.** Not everyone has the same experience level. Help people learn.

**Be respectful.** Disagreements are fine. Personal attacks are not.

## Not Acceptable

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open an issue or contact the maintainers directly.
